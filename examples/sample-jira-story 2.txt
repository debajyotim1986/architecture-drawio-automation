Key: PROJ-123
Type: Story
Title: Add asynchronous order-confirmation email after checkout

Summary
-------
After a customer completes checkout, the system must send a confirmation
email asynchronously. The checkout service should not block on email
delivery, and email failures must not fail the order.

Acceptance criteria
-------------------
- When the Checkout Service finalizes an order, it publishes an
  "OrderPlaced" event to a message broker (Kafka topic
  "orders.placed.v1").
- A new Notification Service consumes "orders.placed.v1" and renders an
  email using a template stored in the existing Template Store
  (PostgreSQL).
- The Notification Service calls SendGrid to deliver the email.
- Failures are retried up to 3 times with exponential backoff, then sent
  to a dead-letter topic "orders.placed.dlq".
- All events are audit-logged to the existing Audit DB.

Out of scope
------------
- SMS or push notifications.
- Internationalization of email content.
